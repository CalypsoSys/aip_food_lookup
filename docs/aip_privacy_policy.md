## Privacy Policy

Effective date: To be determined

AIP Food Lookup helps users check foods against an Autoimmune Protocol food list. The app is informational only and is
not medical advice.

## Information we collect

The app may send the following information to our servers when you use it:

- Food search text that you enter.
- Food suggestions you submit, including the food text and whether you marked it allowed or not allowed.
- Feedback you choose to send, which may include your name, email address, subject, message, and app source.
- Basic app request details such as app client name, app version, request path, response status, and network metadata.

Do not include medical records, health measurements, passwords, payment details, or other sensitive personal information
in food searches, suggestions, or feedback messages.

## How we use information

We use this information to:

- Return food lookup results.
- Review suggested food additions or corrections.
- Respond to support and feedback messages when contact information is provided.
- Monitor reliability, diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and improve the app.
- Maintain and improve the food catalog.

## Feedback and third-party services

Feedback and food suggestions may be delivered to server-side tools such as Slack so the maintainer can review them.
If delivery to Slack is unavailable, the backend may store feedback or suggestions in server-side files for later review.

The app does not put private backend secrets, Slack webhook URLs, or internal API keys on the user's device.

## Advertising

The app may show ads in a future release. If ads are enabled, advertising partners such as Google AdMob may process
device, app, and ad interaction information to serve, measure, and improve ads according to their own policies and user
controls.

We will update this policy and the Google Play Data Safety form before enabling production ads.

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the app,
including hosting, logging, feedback routing, analytics, security, and advertising providers. These providers process
information on our behalf or according to their own published terms.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users, or to protect the security and integrity of the
app and related services.

## Data retention

We keep submitted feedback, suggestions, and operational logs only as long as needed to operate, support, secure, and
improve the app. Retention periods may vary depending on backup, logging, and support workflows.

## Children's privacy

AIP Food Lookup is not directed to children. Users should not submit personal information about children through
feedback or suggestion forms.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect app data. No internet service can guarantee absolute
security.

## Your choices

You can use the food lookup without submitting optional feedback contact information. If you want to request deletion of
feedback or other information you submitted, contact us with enough detail to identify the request.
